  • Advanced Micro Devices (AMD): Significant competitor in the GPU market with its Radeon series, and also competes in the Central Processing Unit (CPU) market with its Ryzen and EPYC processors.
  • Intel Corporation: Dominant player in the CPU market and making aggressive moves to enter the discrete GPU market with its Intel Arc series.
  • Qualcomm: Leader in mobile processors and AI solutions for mobile and edge devices.
  • Apple: Designs its own custom silicon, including the M1 and M2 chips, which offer integrated graphics and AI capabilities.
  • Arm Holdings (and its licensees): Designs the architecture used by many companies for CPUs and GPUs, particularly in mobile and Internet of Things (IoT) devices.
  • Microsoft: Expanding its presence in AI and cloud computing with custom AI chips.
  • Google: Develops custom AI hardware, such as the Tensor Processing Units (TPUs), for its data centers and AI services.
